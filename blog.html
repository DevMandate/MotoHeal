Car Maintenance FAQs

Get answers to common questions about car maintenance. From choosing the right oil to understanding tire wear and battery care, we cover essential information to help you keep your vehicle in top condition.

What type of oil should I use for my car?

▼

The type of oil you should use depends on your car's make and model. Always refer to your vehicle's manual for the recommended oil type and viscosity. Typically, synthetic oil is preferred for its superior performance and protection.

How can I tell if my tires need replacement?

▼

Check your tire tread depth using a tread gauge. If the tread is below 2/32 of an inch, it's time for a replacement. Additionally, look for signs of uneven wear or bulges, which may indicate a need for new tires.

How do I maintain my car battery?

▼

Regularly check your battery terminals for corrosion and clean them if necessary. Ensure your battery is securely mounted and test it regularly to prevent unexpected failures. Consider replacing it every 3-5 years.
